Migration is stuck or slow

If a file is still stored in iCloud, downloading it runs at the speed Apple's iCloud service allows for your account — this is outside the app's control. Once a file is downloaded and copied to your destination, it no longer depends on iCloud.

Migration can be paused and resumed at any time. It always picks up exactly where it left off instead of starting over.

Where do my files go?

PhotoDump copies your photos and videos to a destination you configure yourself: a USB/SSD drive, your own S3-compatible storage, or a REST server you control. Files are only marked safe to delete from your phone after they are confirmed to exist on that destination.

S3 and REST server support

These destinations are labeled Beta and still being refined. USB/SSD is the most stable option today. If you hit an issue with S3 or REST, please include your destination type and app version in your email — Settings → Send Feedback pre-fills this for you.
