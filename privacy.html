1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PhotoDump ("the Application", "we", "us") handles information in connection with your use of the Application. By downloading, installing, or using the Application, you agree to the terms described in this Policy.

2. Information We Access

The Application requests access to your device's Photo Library, subject to your explicit authorization through Apple's system permission prompt, for the sole purpose of identifying and transferring photos and videos you select to a destination you configure.

Where a photo or video contains embedded location metadata, the Application may use Apple's on-device CoreLocation framework to resolve that coordinate into a city or country label, solely to support search and organization features within the Application. This resolution occurs through Apple's system geocoding service and is not performed by, or transmitted to, the Application's developer.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The Application does not collect, transmit, or have access to the content of your photos, videos, or associated metadata. The Application does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking software development kit (SDK). No usage data, device identifiers, or behavioral data are collected by the developer.

4. Destination Data

The Application transfers your photos and videos directly from your device to a destination you configure, which may be a USB or SSD storage device physically connected to your device, an S3-compatible storage service, or a REST server. The developer of the Application does not operate, control, or have access to any such destination, and does not receive a copy of any file transferred.

Where you configure an S3-compatible or REST destination, any access credentials (including access keys, secret keys, or authentication tokens) you provide are stored exclusively within your device's Keychain, a secure storage facility provided by the operating system, and are transmitted only to the destination you specified. Such credentials are not accessible to, or transmitted to, the developer.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Records the Application maintains regarding migration status (such as which files have been transferred) are stored locally on your device and, where applicable, on the destination you configure. The developer does not maintain a copy of this information on any server.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application does not share information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Where the Application interacts with Apple system services (such as Photos, CoreLocation, or iCloud), such interactions are governed by Apple's own privacy practices, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Because the developer does not collect or store your photos, videos, or personal data on any server, there is no developer-held data to access, correct, or delete. You retain full control over your content and any destination you configure at all times.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
