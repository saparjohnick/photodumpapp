Support Privacy Policy

Short version

PhotoDump does not collect, transmit, or sell your photos, videos, or personal data to us or anyone else. There is no account, no sign-up, and no analytics or tracking SDK anywhere in the app.

Where your files go

PhotoDump copies your photos and videos only to a destination you configure yourself: a USB/SSD drive physically connected to your device, your own S3-compatible storage, or a REST server you control. We never receive a copy of any file you migrate — it goes directly from your device to the destination you chose.

Photo Library access

PhotoDump requests access to your Photos library to read the photos and videos you choose to migrate, and to reverse-geocode a photo's embedded GPS location (if any) into a city/country label using Apple's on-device CoreLocation framework — this never leaves your device except through Apple's own system geocoding service, the same one Maps and Photos already use.

Destination credentials

If you connect an S3-compatible or REST destination, any access keys or server details you enter are stored in your device's Keychain and used only to talk to the destination you specified. They are never sent to us.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • No account or sign-up required to use the app
  • No data sold or shared with third parties
  • No tracking across other apps or websites
